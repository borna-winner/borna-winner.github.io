Recipes for health: Ghee

Ghee is not only the tasiest fat, but healthy

Ghee is a class of clarifyed butter orginating from india. In india Ghee is used not only as an amazing flavour and health benefit, but also in indian mythology.

In Hindu mythology, ghee was created by Prajapati, the god of offspring. The myth states that ghee was made when Prajapati rubbed his hands together. He then poured the ghee into the fire, which led to the creation of his offspring. Furthermore, ghee has played a significant role in the caste system of India

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Heat
  • Strainer

Directions

  1. Put your desired amount of butter in the pan, rememeber, butter is 20% water, so you will have 80% ghee from butter gram ammount
  2. Turn heat on medium low for 10 mintues or untill liqud looks golden
  3. Strain and enjoy :)
  4. Storage is as easy as putting it into a container and cooling it in the fridge.
