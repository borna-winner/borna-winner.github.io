Recipes for health: Yogurt

Yogurt is made from... yogurt left overnight in heated to 160 degrees milk.

What came first? the yogurt or the yogurt?

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Heat
  • Yogurt Culture

Directions

  1. Put desired amount of milk into a pot
  2. Turn heat on medium low until milk reaches 160 degress
  3. Let milk cool till you can put ur pinky in for 10 seconds without hurting yourself
  4. Add some of the milk into a small bowl with ur yogurt in it
  5. Mix together and add into big pot of milk
  6. Cover the pot for 6-12 hours
  7. Your yogurt is ready :)
  8. Storage is as easy as putting it into a container and cooling it in the fridge.
