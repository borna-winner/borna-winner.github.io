Recipes for health: Oatmeal

Oatmeal is made from putting milk and oatmeal together into a bowl and heating it up till it is are cooked. Yum

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Heat
  • Old Fashioned Oatmeal

Directions

  1. Take some oatmeal put it into a bowl or pot
  2. Add milk until all oats in ur bowl or pot are floating
  3. Cook for 3 minutes or until desired consistency(Oats thicken infinitly almost!)
  4. Add Ghee and Pink salt to ur oats and mix it around alot!
  5. Let your Oatmeal cool and thicken for 5 minutes
  6. Enjoy your deliciously healthy and great starter for the day breakfast.
