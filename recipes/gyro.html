Recipes for health: Chicken Gyro

Gyros or sometimes gyro is a Greek dish made from meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie. In Greece and Cyprus it is normally made with pork or sometimes with chicken, whlist beef, chicken, and lamb are common in other countries.

If you hadnt had these. oh man.

Ingredients

  • Marinated Greek Chicken
  • Tzatziki
  • Tomato Cucmember salad
  • Flatbread of pita bread
Marinade
  • 3 Large garlic cloves
  • 3 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp extra virigin olive oli
  • 3 Tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 Tsp salt and pepper
Tzatziki
  • 2 Cucumber
  • 1 1/4 cups Yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp extra virigin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove minced
  • black pepper
Salad
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 3 cucumbers
  • 1/2 red spanish onion
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • salt and pepper

Directions

Make the Tazatziki
  1. Place the Marinade ingredients in a ziplock bag and massage to mix. Add the chicken into the ziplock bag and massage to cover all the chicken in the Marinade.
  2. Cut the cucumber in half lengthwise. Use a teaspoon to scrape the watery seeds out. Coarsely grate the cucumber using a box grater. Then wrap in paper towels or a tea towel and squeeze to remove excess liquid.
  3. Place cucumber in a bowl. Add remaining ingredients then mix to combine. Set aside for at least 20 minutes for the flavours to meld.
Cook chicken
  1. Oil on pan or grill.
  2. Remove chicken from Marinade. Cook the chicken for 2 to 3 minutes on each side, until golden brown and cooked through.
  3. Remove the chicken from the grill / fry pan onto a plate. Cover loosely with foil and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving.
Assemble!
  1. Cut chicken into eatable chunks if not already
  2. Get a pita bread or flatbread (preferably warmed) and place it on a piece of parchment (baking) paper (or you could use foil). Place some Salad down the middle of the bread, then top with chicken and Tzatziki.
  3. Roll wrap.
  4. Consume the fruits of your labor.
